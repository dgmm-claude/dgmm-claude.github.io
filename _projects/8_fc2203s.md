> **English summary:** An open-hardware half-bridge brushed-motor driver based on the FC2203S gate driver, released under GPL-3.0 for pumps and small motors. The total bill of materials is about 5 CNY (driver ~0.22 CNY, NMOS ~0.88 CNY, passives ~3 CNY). The design includes gate-protection diodes, a free-wheeling diode, and bulk decoupling — added after debugging early burnout failures documented in a separate note. Measured high-side turn-on is about 400 ns with a 10 Ω gate resistor. Full EasyEDA project files, schematic/PCB PDFs, and measured waveforms are published on GitHub as a reproducible reference design.

基于 FC2203S 芯片的有刷电机半桥驱动开源硬件项目，适用于气泵、小型电机等场景。开源完整的电机驱动设计，整体复刻成本约 **5 元**。已发布于 GitHub。

- **嘉立创工程文件**：可直接导入立创 EDA 编辑打样
- **原理图 & PCB PDF**：便于查看学习
- **实测数据**：10Ω 栅极限流电阻下，上管开启时间约 **400 ns**
